Executive Summary

Elevate Frame Group is a Florida-based, disabled, minority veteran-owned media firm operating at the intersection of narrative production, aerial and terrestrial cinematography, and standards-driven execution.

What distinguishes Elevate Frame Group is not only the range of environments in which it can operate, but the system behind how it works — shaped by military service, technical and aviation training, cinematic production experience, and Embry-Riddle mentorship.

At Elevate Frame Group, all things begin and end with story. Story is the organizing principle that gives the work meaning, sequence, and value — proven in work connected to the rehabilitation of Jayson Williams, later featured on 60 Minutes Sports.

"Imagery without story is noise. Technical imagery without interpretation is dead weight."
Gregory D. Goyins · CEO & Founder
